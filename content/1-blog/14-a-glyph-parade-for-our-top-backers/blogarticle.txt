<em>In 2014, we launched a Kickstarter campain to keep developing Prototypo. It succeeded beyond our expectations, bringing Prototypo around 50000€ (three times the amount aimed!!). Among our 1670 wonderful backers, four guys earned the right to stick their names to a glyph. Here are the four glyphs, all from our last brainchild Prototypo Elzevir!</em>
<strong>Kevin Hohler</strong> sponsored “Ampersand” for Elzevir, a charming glyph with luscious curves. Kevin is a french graphic designer, you can have a look at his work on <a href="http://kevin-hohler.com/" target="_blank">kevin-hohler.com</a>.
